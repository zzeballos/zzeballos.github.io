Art Magazine

One of our major projects in Advanced Design was to design an art/design magazine inspired by a famous designer. I have always been interested in film and motion graphics so I decided to design my magazine based off the works of award winning graphic designer, Pablo Ferro.

Pablo Ferro is known for his title design in classic movies like Dr. Strangelove, The Addams Family, and The Thomas Crown Affair. All of these films and more went into my overall design for my magazine layout. I wanted the magazine to have a strong grounding in film and pay homage to the incredible title designers that inspire me.
