Embrace TikTok Guide

During my time working with Embrace Pet Insurance through Studio 5, one thing the company wanted to work on was their social presence on TikTok. At the time, they were working on hiring a new social media manager so among the social media campaigns my team and I designed, I lead the charge in composing a business guide to TikTok.

For this guide, I cross referenced multiple social media guides to compile helpful information for anyone running the company’s TikTok no matter their experience. This way, the company would not need to waste their time doing outside research and comparing different articles.

Once written, I designed a simple layout for the guide. I wanted to take cues from the company’s style guide and prioritize directness in the document. To do this, I kept the page layout simple with easy to read type and minimal decorations. As this would remain an internal document, extra decorations felt distracting to the main point of the guide.

What is Studio 5?
