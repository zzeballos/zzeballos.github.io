CommWorks Marketing Materials

My main role in my position as the Dean’s Fellow for Simmons University’s Communications Department was to organize and support the annual senior work showcase, CommWorks.

The future of communications (or comm) was used as a theme for the annual senior magazine that year. To create a better unity with the magazine that would be published and distributed at the event, CommWorks promotional materials were designed to match.

Using the futuristic style, I designed a logo, a flyer, a social media square, and various other print signage for the event.
