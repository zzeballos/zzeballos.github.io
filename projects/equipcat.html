CommLab Equipment Catalogue

One of the major projects I worked on as a communications instructional assistant was developing an equipment catalogue for the computer lab. The Ifill College CommLab carries a wide range of equipment available for students taking communications classes so this catalogue was designed to help students be more aware of their resources.

I took over this project from a senior student who designed the general style and most of the graphics. From there, I researched and collected the internal content for the catalogue including photos, names, and serial numbers along with creating additional graphics. Once the contents were collected, I rearranged the catalogue layouts and redesigned as needed to preserve the clean style while also including all the necessary information.
