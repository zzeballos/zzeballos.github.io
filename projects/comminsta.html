Comm Liaison Instagram

During my time at college, I was a part of the Communications Liaison which worked to create community in the communications department. As an organization we put on workshops on relevant topics like graphic design, marketing, and professional development. We also hosted larger form events like a student gallery show and our annual networking dinner.

I joined the team as the social media manager my second year and later would work as the vice president of the organization for my final two years at Simmons University. Throughout all three years, I helped manage the social media account and designed social media posts and print flyers to promote club events.
